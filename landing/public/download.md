## System Requirements

- macOS 13 Ventura or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel
- Claude account (Pro, Max, or Enterprise)

## Install Steps

1. **Download the DMG** from GitHub Releases (link above).
2. **Open the DMG** and drag `Clawdephobia.app` to `/Applications`.
3. **First launch:** right-click → **Open** to clear Gatekeeper. After that, launch normally.

## After Install — Get Your Session Key

1. Open https://claude.ai in your browser.
2. Open DevTools: `Cmd+Option+I`.
3. Go to **Application** tab → **Cookies** → `claude.ai`.
4. Copy the value of `sessionKey` (starts with `sk-ant-sid01-…`).
5. Paste into Clawdephobia.

Your session key is stored in the macOS Keychain. It never leaves your Mac except to authenticate with claude.ai.
